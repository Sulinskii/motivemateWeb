# MotiveMate

## Welcome to the MotiveMate Help Center

Build better habits with less friction. The MotiveMate Help Center is your one stop for guides, FAQs, and tips to get the most out of the app—whether you’re just getting started or fine-tuning your routine.

### What is MotiveMate?

MotiveMate is a simple, focused habit builder that helps you create activities, set smart reminders, and track progress over time. Streaks, insights, and gentle nudges keep you motivated without the noise.

### What you can do
• Create & organize habits – Set frequency, goals, and schedules in seconds.
• Smart reminders – Timely notifications that adapt to your routine.
• Streaks & progress – See your wins with daily check-ins and charts.
• Flexible editing – Update details quickly with an intuitive, card-style editor.
• Privacy by design – Your data stays on your device unless you choose to back it up.

### Quick start

• Add your first habit: Tap “+” name it, and choose when it repeats.
• Enable reminders: Pick times that fit your day.
• Check in daily: Mark it done and watch your streak grow.

### Troubleshooting

• Not receiving reminders? Check system notification settings and Focus/Do Not Disturb.
• Missed a day by accident? Learn how to adjust entries responsibly without breaking momentum.
